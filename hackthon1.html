About Our University

Founded in 1995, the University of Advanced Sciences has been at the forefront of higher education, innovation, and research for nearly four decades.

We offer over 120 undergraduate and graduate programs across 15 departments, with state-of-the-art facilities and world-class faculty.

Our campus spans 200 acres with modern classrooms, research centers, libraries, and recreational facilities to provide a holistic learning environment.
